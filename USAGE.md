# 📖 UniManage: Comprehensive Usage Guide & Feature Catalog

This document provides a deep dive into the specific features and workflows available within the UniManage platform. Each section is tailored to the specific role-based portals.

---

## 🧑‍🎓 Student Portal: "Your Academic Journey"

The Student Portal is designed to provide complete transparency into a student's academic and financial life.

### 🏠 Dashboard
- **Academic Vitals**: Real-time display of GPA, Attendance Rate, and total Credits earned.
- **Absence Monitoring**: A dynamic risk indicator that highlights subjects where absences are approaching the 25% limit.
- **Pulse Schedule**: View today's classes with room numbers and instructor details at a glance.

### 📚 Academic Management
- **My Curriculum**: View the full list of semester subjects, including retakes (Kəsir) and their current status.
- **Detailed Subject Pages**: Deep dive into any subject to see session-by-session presence logs and activity point breakdowns.
- **Grades & GPA**: A complete history of semester results with weighted GPA calculations.
- **Attendance Registry**: A detailed table of every lesson, marked as Present (i.e) or Absent (q.b).
  - *Participation Rule*: Activity points are only visible for sessions where participation was confirmed (any non-zero score).


### 💰 Finance & Operations
- **Finance Hub**: Track tuition fees, scholarships (General vs. Special), and current outstanding balances.
- **Exam Center Center**: Monitor upcoming exam schedules, hall assignments, and download exam entrance permits.
- **Formal Requests**: Submit digital applications for academic documents or record corrections directly to the administration.
- **Academic History**: A chronological log of every semester completed at the university.

## 👩‍🏫 Teacher Portal: "The Educator's Command Center"

The Teacher Portal focuses on efficient classroom management and institutional reporting.

### 📝 Classroom Operations
- **Smart Attendance**: A time-sensitive engine to mark student presence directly within the Class Workspace.
  - *Rule*: Attendance must be finalized before any daily activity points can be entered.
- **Journal Creation**: Dedicated workflows to initiate Kollekvium and Sərbəst İş assessment sessions.
  - *Rule*: In creation mode, students are either graded or marked as **q.b** (Absent). No "i.e" placeholders are used for formal assessments.
- **Student Performance List**: View group-wide averages and identify students requiring academic intervention.


### 📊 Reporting & Analytics
- **Teacher Schedule**: Interactive weekly timetable with group and room identifiers.
- **Academic Reports**: Automated generation of terminal performance reports for the Faculty Board.
- **Management Console**: Sync journals with university servers and manage personal academic credentials.

## 👔 Dept Head Portal: "Governance & Quality Control"

Tools for managing faculty performance and department-wide academic standards.

### 👥 Staff Management
- **Teacher Assignments**: Link professors to specific subjects and student groups based on departmental needs.
- **Faculty Teaching Load**: Monitor real-time charts showing which teachers are within "Normal" limits and who is in "Overload".

### 🎓 Curriculum Oversight
- **Subject Mastery**: Manage the catalog of subjects offered by the department.
- **Group Monitoring**: View the collective performance of student cohorts (e.g., CS-640A).

---

## 🏢 Admin Portal: "University Global Operations"

The central registry for all university-wide data and institutional configurations.

### 🏛️ Institutional Directory
- **Student Database**: Manage all enrolled students, their status (Active, On-Leave, Expelled), and contact records.
- **Faculty & Dept List**: Structural management of the university's hierarchy.
- **User Management**: Creating and disabling accounts for staff and university personnel.

### ⚙️ System Configuration
- **Academic Calendar**: Defining the year, semester dates, and official holidays.
- **Attendance Registry Rules**: Configuring the global thresholds for absence warnings.
- **Correction Processing**: Approving or rejecting requests for record changes submitted by teachers.

---

## 🛠️ IT Admin (SuperAdmin): "Infrastructure & Integrity"

Deep system tools to ensure 100% uptime and data security.

### 🛡️ Security & Logs
- **System Vital Signs**: Real-time HUD showing CPU load, RAM usage, and active user sessions.
- **Audit Trails**: Complete logs of who accessed what data and when.
- **Security Protocols**: Management of encryption keys and role permissions.

### 💾 Data Management
- **Backup Suite**: Manual and automated database backup tools.
- **Terminal Access**: Advanced database optimization and scripting tools for emergency maintenance.

---

## 🤝 Tutor Portal: "The Mentorship Bridge"

Focused tools for student success and administrative coordination.

### 🧡 Group Support
- **Focused Monitoring**: Access to performance and attendance data for specifically assigned student groups.
- **Early Warning System**: Automatic notification when a student flags for academic risk.

---

## ⌨️ Common Portal Workflows

1. **Changing Your Workspace**: Use the logout button or click the "UniManage" logo in the sidebar to return to the Role Selection screen.
2. **System Health Check**: The green "System Online" pulse in the top bar indicates a stable connection to the university database.
3. **Formal Communication**: Use the "Requests" module found in Student and Teacher sidebars to communicate with the registry.
